About HassanaLabs

HassanaLabs is named in memory of Hassana, whose resilience and compassion inspire our work. We strive to unite scientific innovation with community empowerment across the Levant and beyond.

Our values center on inclusivity, open knowledge, and using technology for social good.

Milestones

  • 2023 – HassanaLabs founded.
  • 2024 – AI-driven drug discovery pipeline established.
  • 2024 – Three papers published inviting contributions from underrepresented backgrounds.
  • 2024 – Workshops delivered to Gulf women and displaced people in Lebanon.

Team

Led by Leo Chlon with collaborators, advisors, and volunteers dedicated to advancing science and opportunity.
