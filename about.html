About HassanaLabs

HassanaLabs is named in memory of Hassana, whose resilience and compassion inspire our work. We strive to unite scientific innovation with community empowerment across the Levant and beyond.

Our values center on inclusivity, open knowledge, and using technology for social good.

Milestones

2023 · HassanaLabs founded

Established to honor Hassana’s legacy by bridging research and community.

2024 · AI drug discovery pipeline

Built an AI-enhanced pipeline targeting neglected diseases.

2024 · Open research & papers

Published calls for contributions from underrepresented backgrounds.

2024 · Workshops & mentorship

Hands-on ML sessions for Gulf women and displaced communities in Lebanon.
