Programs & Initiatives

We design accessible programs that make advanced technology and career guidance available to underrepresented communities.

Hands-on workshops introducing core ML concepts. Ideal for beginners and delivered with a focus on practical skills.

Who for: Women in the Gulf, displaced people in Lebanon, and anyone seeking inclusive tech education.

How to participate: Stay tuned for upcoming sessions or contact us to request a workshop.

Guidance on navigating tech and science careers, from CV reviews to interview preparation.

Highlights: Free seminars in London and remote mentoring for students worldwide.
